Hi! I am Chaoyi Chen!


I’m a PhD candidate in the Rutgers Department of Linguistics.
I am interested in Syntax, Semantics, and their interfaces. Language-wise, I specialize in English, Mandarin Chinese and Fuzhou Chinese .
My doctoral dissertation investigates the syntax and semantics of free relatives, co-supervised by Mark Baker and Yimei Xiang.
Other than free relatives, I am also interested in concessive scalar particles, verbal reduplications, limited ellipsis, null categories, and reflexives.



Recent activities:

2023, August 17-19. The antecedent preferences for Mandarin reflexive ziji and ta-ziji. Poster presentation at ICTEAP-4.

2023, May 12-14. The object-reading preference in doubly-gapped Mandarin relatives. Oral presentation at TEAL-13.
