Hi! I am Chaoyi Chen!


I’m a PhD candidate in the Rutgers Department of Linguistics.
I am interested in Syntax, Semantics, and their interfaces. Language-wise, I specialize in English, Mandarin Chinese and Fuzhou Chinese .
My doctoral dissertation investigates the syntax and semantics of free relatives, co-supervised by Mark Baker and Yimei Xiang.



Recent activities:

2023, April 28-30. Concessiveness and verum focus: the emergence of Mandarin concessive scalar particle hai. Oral presentation at CLS-59. the University of Chicago, Chicago.

2023, May 12-14. The object-reading preference in doubly-gapped Mandarin relatives. Oral presentation at TEAL-13. National Taiwan Normal University, Taipei.
